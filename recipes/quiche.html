World's best quiche

This quiche recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Cheese
  • Onion
  • Pie crust
  • Cream
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Cook, chop, and drain the bacon
  2. Sprinkle the bacon, cheese, and onion in the shell
  3. Mix the ingredients and seasonings
  4. Pour the mixture into the shell
  5. Bake until a knife comes out clean
